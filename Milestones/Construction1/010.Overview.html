Overview

This section give an overview of the contents of the Construction 1 document

The documentation completed during the Construction 1 is to help the team begin the initial stages of implementing our plan, and creating our project. A successful Construction 1 document will include the following:

  • An initial users guide
  • Completed use case models
  • Glossory
  • Completed system architecure
  • Completed UML diagrams
  • Working applciation code
  • Test code
  • Contribution summary

At the end of the Construction 1 we should have a better understanding of construction needs, issues, and barriers.
